The usual sequences unmoderated online usability tests from the point of view of the user.

- Screener (Advance questions to determine whether the person fits the target group)
- Advance questions on user habits & prior knowledge
- Start of video recording
- Processing of tasksPost-task question(s)
- Uploading of video(s)
- Information or transfer incentive/payment
